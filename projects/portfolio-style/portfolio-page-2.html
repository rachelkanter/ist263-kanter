Graphic Design Samples

All the content below is from my VIS 207 (graphic design) class.

Resume Project

The Resume Project was dedicated to revamping our resumes. The styling elements that I added include choosing new fonts, fixing the layout of my content and including a logo which is feautred at the top of the page.

Campaign Project

The purpose of this project was to pick a non-profit and create a poster around one of their campaigns. The non-profit that is featured is the Food Bank of Central New York. The event on the poster is a food bank around halloween time which is represented by the jack-o-lantern.

Branding Project

The Branding Project was teaching us about how to work with a client and tailor logos, fonts and other styling elements to the client. I was assigned Edgar Allan Poe. One of Poe's most famous poems is "The Raven." The logo features the Raven as well as the profile of a man, showing how Poe is intertwined with his life's work. The fonts and other elements were intended to show the creepy and mysterious nature of Poe and his work.

Magazine Project

The final project in VIS 207 was to create our own magazine. I based my magazine on fall food. The assignment called for a magazine cover, an opening spread and the article page which were all designed by me.
