The Resume Project focused on revamping our previous resumes to fit our own unique styles. Each resume required new fonts and a personal logo. The logo featured at the top of this website is the logo highlighted on my resume. The fonts used on my website are also in my resume.

Campaign Project

The purpose of this project was to pick a non-profit and create a poster around one of their campaigns. The non-profit that is featured is the Food Bank of Central New York. The event on the poster is a food bank around halloween time which is represented by the jack-o-lantern.

Branding Project

Previous Next

The Branding Project taught us about how to work with a client and tailor logos, fonts and other styling elements to the client. I was assigned Edgar Allan Poe. One of Poe's most famous poems is "The Raven." The logo features the Raven as well as the profile of a man, showing how Poe is intertwined with his life's work. The fonts and other elements were intended to show the creepy and mysterious nature of Poe and his work.
